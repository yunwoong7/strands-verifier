RFP A-123 Version 3 - Power Management System Requirements
=======================================================

1. General Requirements
=======================

1.1 Operating Environment
The power management system shall operate reliably in industrial automotive environments.

1.2 Temperature Requirements
Operating temperature range: -40°C to 80°C
Storage temperature range: -55°C to 105°C
Thermal cycling: 1000 cycles minimum

1.3 Electrical Specifications
Input voltage: 9V to 16V DC
Output voltage: 5V ±5% and 3.3V ±3%
Maximum power consumption: 150W
Efficiency: minimum 85%

2. Physical Requirements
========================

2.1 Dimensions
Maximum dimensions: 120mm x 80mm x 25mm
Weight: maximum 200g

2.2 Connectors
Automotive grade connectors required
IP65 rating minimum for all external connections

2.3 Materials
Heat sink material: Aluminum 6061-T6 preferred
PCB material: FR4 with minimum thickness 1.6mm

3. Safety Requirements
======================

3.1 Isolation
Galvanic isolation between input and output
Isolation voltage: minimum 1500V AC

3.2 Protection
Overcurrent protection: implemented
Overvoltage protection: implemented
Thermal protection: automatic shutdown at 95°C

4. Testing Requirements
=======================

4.1 Environmental Testing
Temperature cycling per IEC 60068-2-14
Vibration testing per ISO 16750-3
EMC testing per CISPR 25

4.2 Electrical Testing
Load regulation: within ±2%
Line regulation: within ±1%
Ripple and noise: less than 50mVpp
